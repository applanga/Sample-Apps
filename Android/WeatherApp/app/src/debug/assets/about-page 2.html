Weather-App

Weather-App is a simple tool to help you find out what's the weather like in your desired location

Features

Get a detailed report of the weather in your current location or get a daily temperature report by time and date

Displayed information is based on the location (city based) you provide

Display

A simple UI designed to give you the most important information in the easiest way

Settings

Use the settings window to change the displayed location, preferred measurement unit, app language and number of displayed days
